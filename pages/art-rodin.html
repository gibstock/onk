Andre Gonzales

Monday Dec 30th, 2019

Auguste Rodin

By Andre Gonzales

Pen on Paper

11 x 8.5 in.

2005


I can remember wandering the walls and halls of the greats, knowing I was supposed to be there. Both in time and in space. For reasons I'd learn 11 years in the future. Hindsight 2020.

This was a study of the Rodin statue in the courtyard of the Legion of Honor in San Francisco. It was just after I moved from Sacramento to San Francisco and I felt excited that a fantastic voyage had begun.
