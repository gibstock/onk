Hide and Sleep

By Andre Gonzales

Acrylic on Paper

2016


I am a Snorlax. My powers are plenty and near.

Sometimes I can't remember where, why or when I made a piece. So when I find it in my stacks I have the pleasure of experiencing it as an observer.
