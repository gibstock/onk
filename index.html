Chloe Peak

Monday Dec 30th, 2019

The Vineyard

By Chloe Peak

Mixed Media Recycled Plastic and Acrylic on Bristol

29 x 24 in.

2019


Empty little bags of mine
Strips of plastic city
Green and nitty gritty
From the hills we drink the wine

Take what you get
As we expand
Don't be upset
Live hand and hand

The Vineyard was made with recycled bags that I have accumulated from dispensaries cut up into little buildings. I love how we make are mark as humans but any time we delay something from going to a landfill is a positive action for Earth.
