This is The Duke of Raley's

As I was waiting for my ride after a long work shift, I saw this gentleman float past me and cross the street towards Raley's.

When he was mid street, he stopped and turned his head and shoulders to face me and said " ".

Even though no words came out, I knew what he meant. He needed to pick up some bacon and eggs.

It was in that moment that I realized, it was time to wake up. And go to work.
